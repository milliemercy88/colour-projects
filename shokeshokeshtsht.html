Flowers Whether in a boquet or growing in a pot or flower bed,always have a special wau of lifting our sprits.This is due in part to the principles of colour therapy,an alternative therapy grounded in the idea that colours have powerful effects on our mood.You can combine the wellness benefits of gardening,with some colour therapy,to get the best of both worlds Add a few of these colourful flowers to your des moines garden to make you,and your neighbours,smile
